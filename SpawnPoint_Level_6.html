🎯 GUIDED PROBLEM 1: VOLUME (8.7A)

The volume of a cone is approximately 1,647 cubic centimeters. The height of the cone is 13 centimeters. Which measurement is closest to the diameter of the base of the cone in centimeters?
VOLUME OF CONE: V = 13Bh where B = πr²

STEP 1: Type Volume Formula

In line 1, type: V ~ 1/3Bh

(Type "/" creates vertical fraction)

STEP 2: Define Base Area

In line 2, type: B = πr²

STEP 3: Assign Height

In line 3, type: h = 13

STEP 4: Assign Volume

In line 4, type: V = 1647

STEP 5: Read Radius

Look at line 1 - Desmos calculated r = ?

STEP 6: Calculate Diameter

Question asks for diameter (not radius)!

In line 5, type: r · 2
