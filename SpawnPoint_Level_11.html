📍 TEKS 8.5(C) - SCATTER PLOTS & PREDICTIONS

A store tracked temperature and ice cream sales over several days.

Based on the scatterplot, approximately how much in sales on a 95°F day?

💡 CRITICAL: Enter ALL 6 data points from the graph to get an accurate prediction!

🎮 GUIDED WALKTHROUGH

Use Linear Regression to make predictions!

STEP 1: Create a Table with ALL Points

In Desmos, click + button and add a table

Enter ALL 6 data points from the graph:

x₁: 65, 70, 75, 80, 85, 90

y₁: 125, 150, 185, 210, 245, 270

💡 x₁ = temperature, y₁ = sales in dollars

STEP 2: Add Linear Regression

In the blue pane on the left, click the regression button

Desmos will automatically find the line of best fit!

You should see an equation like: y = 6x - 270

STEP 3: Make a Prediction for 95°F

We want to find sales for 95°F

In a new line, type: x₁ = 95

Desmos will show you the y-value on the regression line!

💡 Or use your equation: y = 6(95) - 270

FINAL QUESTION:

Approximately how much in sales on a 95°F day?

✓ PERFECT!

B) $300

Using y = 6(95) - 270 = 570 - 270 = $300!
